Fiscal Indicators

Definition: Fiscal indicators are metrics used to measure the financial health and policies of a government, including revenue collection, expenditure, and debt levels.

Importance:

Fiscal indicators help policymakers, economists, and analysts understand the government's financial position, assess fiscal sustainability, and inform policy decisions.

Fiscal Balance (% of GDP)
