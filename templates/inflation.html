Inflation Indicators

Definition: Inflation indicators provide insights into the rate at which the general level of prices for goods and services is rising, and subsequently, how purchasing power is falling.

Importance:

Inflation indicators are crucial for understanding the economic environment, as they affect the cost of living, purchasing power, and economic policy decisions.
