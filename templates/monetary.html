Monetary Indicators

Definition: Monetary indicators provide insights into the financial health and stability of an economy, focusing on aspects such as interest rates, money supply, and credit.

Importance:

Monetary indicators are crucial for understanding the financial stability and liquidity in the economy. They influence policy decisions, investment strategies, and economic forecasting.
